About

One should have a hobby. Hobbies are a great way to spend your leisure time.



My hobby is to collect buttons of different shapes, sizes and colours. I have a collection of buttons, from coats, pants, shirts and skirts. Some are big as a 1-rupee coin and my smallest one has diameter of 5 millimetres. Some are metal and most are plastic. My collection is colourful, starting from red and ending with purple. Big ones have 4 or 3 holes and smaller ones have 2. I have a rare collection of a pair of wooden buttons. I keep them in small wooden boxes. I made this collection in the span of 2 years. I traded them with my friends to enhance my collection for a mutual benefit. A few of my friends also have this hobby.
